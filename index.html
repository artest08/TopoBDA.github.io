Abstract

This work presents TopoBDA, a novel approach for multi-view 3D object detection that incorporates topology-aware bird's eye view attention mechanisms. Our method enhances the understanding of spatial relationships in autonomous driving scenarios by leveraging topological information to improve detection accuracy and efficiency.
